The Philadelphia School District is interested in encouraging its students to bike to school. The district wants to know what neighborhoods contain its largest (greater than 1200 enrollment) schools that are well-connected (within 0.1 miles) to the bike network. The following five Philadelphia district schools meet these criteria: Baldi, C. C. A.
